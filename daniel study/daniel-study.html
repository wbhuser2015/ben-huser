Daniel Study

A focused Bible study on the Book of Daniel with discussion questions, historical context, and teaching notes for groups and personal growth.

Study Overview

This study explores the major themes of Daniel, including faithfulness under pressure, God’s sovereignty, prophetic vision, and the hope of God’s kingdom.

What’s included

  • Chapter summaries for Daniel 1–12
  • Discussion questions for personal reflection or group study
  • Historical background and interpretive notes
  • Application prompts for daily life and ministry

Weekly Lessons

Week 1: Daniel 1

Faithfulness in exile and staying true to God in a foreign culture.

Week 2: Daniel 2

God’s sovereignty revealed through Nebuchadnezzar’s dream.

Week 3: Daniel 3

Standing firm in faith before the fiery furnace.

Week 4: Daniel 4

Humility, pride, and God’s kingly authority in Nebuchadnezzar’s story.

Week 5: Daniel 5

God’s judgment on Belshazzar and the writing on the wall.

Week 6: Daniel 6

Prayer, persecution, and deliverance from the lions’ den.

Week 7: Daniel 7

The vision of four beasts and the coming kingdom of God.

Week 8: Daniel 8–12

Prophetic visions of the end times, faithfulness, and God’s ultimate victory.

Use This Study

Ideal for Sunday School classes, Bible study groups, church leaders, and individuals seeking a deeper understanding of Daniel’s message.
